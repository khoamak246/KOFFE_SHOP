Our Story

Our story begins in 1971 along the cobblestone streets of Seattle’s historic Pike Place Market.

It was here where KOFFE opened its first store, offering fresh-roasted coffee beans, tea and spices from around the world for our customers to take home. Our name was inspired by the classic tale, “Moby-Dick,” evoking the seafaring tradition of the early coffee traders.
